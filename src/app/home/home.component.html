Most Recent/ Current Series

During quarantine and this most recent pandemic, I have heard and seen the importance of small businesses. This series focuses on local small businesses around Pittsburgh. From the perspective of interiors/atmosphere – I want the viewer to feel as if they are truly there. And when they walk away from each painting, it will leave them with a memory as if they’ve been there. My intent is to provide a sense of familiarity, warmth, and comfortability- something corporations can’t offer. All in hopes to initiate customers to consider shopping small more often or supporting places they otherwise wouldn’t have thought of.
